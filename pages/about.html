About Southern CGS

Specialist gas equipment supplier — bringing precision, purity, and reliability to industries worldwide.

Who We Are

Our Story

Southern Central Gas Suppliers was founded with a clear mission: to provide UK industries with the highest quality gas regulation equipment, backed by genuine technical expertise. As authorised distributors of Rotarex and Wescol — manufacturers with over 100 years of combined heritage — we bring world-class products to your doorstep.

We specialise in three purity grades — industrial, high purity, and ultra-high purity — serving sectors from oil & gas to semiconductor manufacturing.

Meet the Founder

Alvin Gibson

Founder & Managing Director

With years of hands-on experience in the gas equipment industry, Alvin founded Southern Central Gas Suppliers to bridge the gap between world-class manufacturers and UK businesses that need reliable, high-quality gas regulation equipment.

His direct relationships with manufacturers like Rotarex and Wescol mean SCGS customers get not just products, but genuine technical guidance from someone who understands the applications inside and out.
